TimeLine of 21st Century Germany History

  1. 01/01/2002 - Euro was introduced in Germany
  2. 19/04/2005 - Pope Benedict XVI was elected pope.
  3. 09/06/2006 - 2006 FIFA World Cup: The 2006 FIFA World Cup was held in Germany.
  4. 14/09/2008 - Sebastian Vettel wins the Italian Grand Prix, marking him F1's youngest winner.
  5. 14/11/2010 - Sebastian Vettel wins the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix and the Championship, marking him F1's youngest world champion.
  6. 13/07/2014 - 2014 FIFA World Cup Final: Germany defeated the Argentine national team in the final match of the FIFA World Cup in extra time in Rio de Janeiro.
